Community pulse — rated 2.92/5 on Phish.net (50+ ratings the night of the show). The reviews split along one line: the storm. Nearly everyone points to Ghost as the night's peak — a jam soundtracked by actual thunder and lightning over the lawn, which one reviewer called "the absolute perfectly chaotic vibe." The frustration is with what the weather stole: a second set that was building momentum got cut off mid-flight, and some wished for a bigger closer than S.A.N.T.O.S. The playing itself drew praise — Ocelot, Mull, and Sigma Oasis singled out as unusually tight. Read the full reviews on Phish.net.
